Imaginary Ice Cream

A hot summer day always welcomes a cold ice cream but your diet does not! good for you that researchers at nasa have spent years working on an ice cream for the exact situation.

With this you may never have the life long traumatic experience of an ice cream cone sliping through your hand, falling on the ice cream side! All hail ice cream researchers at NASA!

Ingredients-

  • Will to eat ice cream
  • Imaginative/border line delusional mind
  • Imaginary whipping cream
  • Imaginary sugar
  • Imaginary vanilla flavouring
  • Imaginary condensed milk
  • Imaginary chocolate chips
  • REAL ice cream cone

Steps-

  1. Whip the imaginary cream
  2. Mix the imaginary sugar
  3. Mix the imaginary vanilla flavouring agent
  4. Add the imaginary condensed milk
  5. Whip again
  6. Add imaginary chocolate chips and mix lightly
  7. Freeze
  8. serve on an ice cream cone
