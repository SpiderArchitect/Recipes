AIR FLAVOUR CAKE

Everyone loves a good cake! Here is my great grand mother's uncle's recipe which I have perfected over a span of 10 years.

Ingredients-

  • Air all purpose flour
  • Air Butter
  • Air Milk
  • Air Icing Sugar
  • Air Baking powder
  • Air salt
  • Air vanilla essence
  1. Mix all purpose air flour with icing air sugar, pinch of air salt and baking powder.
  2. Mix Melted Air Butter and above mixture.
  3. Mix above mixture and air milk and Air vanilla essence.
  4. carefully pour mixture in an oil covered cake pan.
  5. Bake!!
